Our Products & Services

You can purchase the Vehicle Management Software as a standalone version - you don't have to buy the entire Human Resource System. The Vehicle Management Software runs online, which allows you to access any information from anywhere in the world at any given time, as long as you have Internet access. The Vehicle Management Software is fully customizable to suit the needs of your business. The Vehicle Management Software integrates with other systems such as other Human Resource Software solutions and the Payroll Software. When combining a competent web based Vehicle Management Software with other systems you ensure that your company has a dynamic process integrated with the daily management operations you can even integrate with third party systems available to the fleet manager, in order to automate the processes. These systems include vehicle tracking systems, computerised clocking systems, on-board load cells and general driving technique information systems. Reliable Vehicle Management Software includes well defined written policies and formal processes in the vehicle operating environment.
